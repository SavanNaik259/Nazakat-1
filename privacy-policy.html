Privacy Policy

Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information at Nazakat.

Effective Date: August 18, 2025

Nazakat values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Name, email, phone number, billing and shipping address.
  • Payment details (processed securely via third-party gateways).
  • Browsing data through cookies (for a better shopping experience).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To communicate regarding order status, promotions, or customer support.
  • To improve our website and user experience.

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be shared only with trusted third parties such as:

  • Payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, PayPal).
  • Delivery partners for shipping.
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel) for performance tracking.

4. Data Security

  • Our website uses SSL encryption and secure servers to protect your information.
  • Payment details are handled directly by secure third-party gateways.

5. User Rights

  • You may request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may opt out of promotional emails anytime by unsubscribing.

6. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Keep items in your shopping cart.
  • Improve browsing experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and marketing performance.

7. Updates

Nazakat reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page.
